Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ugazo ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). Ugazo is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an account (email, password, name, age, gender, country, phone number)
  • Complete your profile (bio, interests, photos, videos)
  • Send messages or love requests
  • Make payments
  • Contact our support team

We automatically collect certain technical information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Process payments
  • Send notifications about matches and requests
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Enforce our terms and conditions
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve user experience

4. Email Privacy

Your email address is never displayed in your public profile. We keep email addresses confidential and only use them for:

  • Account login and verification
  • Password recovery
  • Important notifications
  • Match notifications

5. Photo and Video Privacy

Your photos and videos are stored securely and only visible to:

  • Other users searching for matches
  • Users you have matched with

You can delete any photo or video at any time from your profile.

6. Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe, a third-party payment processor. We do NOT store credit card details. All payments are encrypted and secured following PCI DSS standards.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, hosting providers)
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users (only your public profile information)

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Password hashing (bcryptjs)
  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile will be removed from search results
  • Your personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request a copy of your data

11. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

13. Children's Privacy

Ugazo is not intended for anyone under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover such information, we will delete it immediately.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Ugazo Support
Email: privacy@ugazo.com
Response time: Within 7 business days

15. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominent notification on our website.
